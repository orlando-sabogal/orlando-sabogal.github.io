Climate change and the reduction of inequalities are two of the most important challenges of our time. Directly or indirectly my work turns around these two themes.

I am engaged to study urban development with a focus on understanding the links among transportation and social phenomena. Moreover, I am interested in explore their geographic dimension and in applying structural equation models SEM. By and large, the main tool I use is the R programming language. I am very keen towards open source and reproducible research.
